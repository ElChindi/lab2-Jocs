Controllers:

  (Keyboard |  Joystick  )

When being the pirate:

- (  WASD   | Left stick ): move player
- ( Arrows  | Right stick): move camera
- (  Space  |      X     ): attack
- (    E    |      Y     ): get on the ship
- (    Q    |      B     ): dodge

When being on the ship:

- (   A-D   | Left stick ): rotate ship
- (    W    |      A     ): accelerate
- (    S    |      B     ): break
- (    E    |      Y     ): go down to isle (should be still)

Miscellaneous:

- (    P    |    Start   ): pause
